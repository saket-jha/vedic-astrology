BIODATA Of Vinay Jha (summary ) A lifelong brahmachāri ( student ), software developer, astrologer, weather scientist, etc.

Qualifications :

  • Topped in science subjects in middle board (class-viii) of CBSE in 1973 in Delhi. Studied science.
  • Then topped in English (Hons) in 1980-82 batch, LN Mithila University.
  • Thereafter took refuse from mundane (selfish) works, became a recluse.
  • Studied privately ( esp. Comparative Linguistics, Jyotisha, Philosophy, etc) from Late Dr Lakshamana Jha (Ph.D., School of Oriental and African Studies or SOAS, London ), former Vice Chancellor of LN Mithilā University and a Bāla-brahmachāri Sādhu.

Experience and Works :

  • Secretary of Sanskrit Mahavidyalaya (recognized for Prathamaa to Achaarya), Barha, via Benipatti, District Madhubani, Bihar, India. सचिव, संस्कृत महाविद्यालय(प्रथमा से आचार्य तक मान्यताप्राप्त , जहां एक गुरुकुल / ब्रह्मचर्याश्रम भी है), बरहा, बेनीपट्टी, जिला मधुबनी, बिहार, भारत।
  • Trustee of JB Brahmacharyaasshrama, Lagma, District Darbhanga, Bihar, India, which manages two recognized Sanskrit colleges, two recognized Sanskrit schools and one Brahmacharyaashrama for Vedic students with free food and lodging.
  • Decision by KSD Sanskrit University, Bihar, India in April 2007 to rectify its almanac (Panchaanga) with the help of software developed by Vinay Jhā (after a decision by Patna High Court): http://jyotirvidya.wikifoundry.com/page/Credentials
